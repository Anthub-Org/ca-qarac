Collision paperwork

Bring the repair facts together before approving work.

For insured collision repairs, bring the claim number, adjuster contact, photos, and any notes from your insurer. If your policy uses a preferred repair network or Direct Repair Program (DRP), ask how the estimate, approvals, and paperwork should be coordinated.

Helpful to have ready

  • Vehicle make, model, year, and VIN if available.
  • Photos of the damage from a few angles.
  • Insurance claim number or adjuster contact.
  • Any warning lights or changes after the collision.
